PERFORMANCE

I believe that the success of any business ultimately depends on the performance of its employees. Companies that truly understand this focus not just on profits, but also on how productive and efficient their teams are. Productivity doesn’t just stay at one level — it multiplies. When employees at the ground level perform well, it creates a ripple effect across the entire organization. That’s why I feel it's extremely important to analyze and understand employee performance in any company. This is what motivated me to work on this project.
